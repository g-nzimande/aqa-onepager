AQA — “Akha” / “Aga” • means “build”

An AI-powered learning hub for LGBTIQ+ refugees & asylum seekers

AQA curates language and skills courses, connects learners to inclusive tutors & organisations, and personalises pathways to earn fast or build long-term — all with no documentation required.

Partner with us Explore features

Pilot implemented with The Fruit Basket • Founder retains IP

The Challenge

Talent exists everywhere. Opportunity does not.

Queer and displaced Africans face systemic barriers to education: missing IDs, discrimination, language hurdles, and platforms that don’t reflect their realities.

Documentation barriers

Formal education often demands IDs. AQA starts with access first.

Cultural safety

Learners need affirming, identity-safe spaces. AQA verifies all tutors & partners.

Language & context

Courses rarely localise to host-country realities. AQA fixes that.

Our Solution

Personalised learning without borders

AI-driven recommendations connect learners to language teachers, skills trainers and inclusive organisations.

  1. 1) Goal intake: earn now or grow long-term.
  2. 2) AI curation: courses matched by level, goals & location.
  3. 3) Safe onboarding: pseudonyms welcome.
  4. 4) Ongoing guidance: adaptive support and progression.
  • • Language exchange & host-country integration
  • • Digital & financial literacy
  • • Gig-readiness & micro-entrepreneurship
  • • Partner organisation webinars

Roadmap

From pilot to policy

We start where the need and networks are strongest, and scale with partners.

  • MVP launch (web-based)
  • 1,000 learners onboarded

Phase 2 — Scale (10–18m)

  • AI tutor & native app
  • Regional expansion

Phase 3 — Policy (18–36m)

  • Registration body integrations
  • Pan-African rollout
